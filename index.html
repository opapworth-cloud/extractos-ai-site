Mission

Help teams skip the expensive lessons Oregon already learned.

We have watched the Oregon cannabis industry mature through early energy, fast builds, thin systems, and margin compression.

As markets tighten, the separation becomes clear: companies with real operating discipline keep improving while companies that are still improvising get exposed.

ExtractOS exists to bring those lessons to operators before they repeat the same mistakes. When a market matures, mature companies are the only ones that survive. That means teams are usually more under the gun than they realize.

Our mission

Our method

Build the company the market will eventually require.

We start with the mistakes that show up again and again in maturing cannabis markets, then design the operation around avoiding them before they show up as missed revenue, wasted labor, failed batches, or expensive rework.

01

Pressure-test the plan

We challenge throughput assumptions, staffing models, room flow, SKU selection, and market expectations before capital gets trapped in the wrong build or equipment stack.

02

Design around bottlenecks

Facility planning connects equipment, compliance, cleaning, storage, labor, and actual production rhythm so the room can move product instead of just house machinery.

03

Treat cultivation as input control

Extraction performance starts upstream. We help align strain selection, harvest timing, biomass quality, and input consistency with the products the market will reward.

04

Train for pressure

Operators need more than SOP binders. We use SOPs and work instructions to optimize new facilities, then support the handoff with training, run discipline, maintenance planning, and decision habits that hold up on busy days.

05

Instrument the business

Software has to show what is happening on the floor: yields, downtime, inventory, labor, loss, and the few numbers that determine whether the operation is healthy. We integrate SOPs into tools each operator can access, with remote technical support to help keep performance on track.
